Repacking Services



We repack according to your specific instructions.

We offer repacking service to our farmers and importers who want their products to be perfect. We understand what you think and that is why we create an temperature controlled area of 1000 square feet for repacking at our facility.

We do ice adding, re-boxing, re-bagging, tagging, labeling, weighing and sorting and any customized service.
